\section{Conclusions}
\label{sec:conclusions}

We have conducted a spectroscopic multiplicity survey of \Nstars
apparently single WC/WO stars in the Large Magellanic Cloud using
multi-epoch VLT/X-SHOOTER spectroscopy. Our main findings are:

\begin{enumerate}
\item We detect \Ndetected new spectroscopic binaries based on
  radial-velocity variability of the \primaryline emission line, bringing
  the total number of confirmed WC/WO binaries in the LMC to \Nbin out of
  \Ntotal ($\fbinobs$).

\item Through Monte-Carlo bias-correction simulations following the
  methodology of \citet{Dsilva2023}, we constrain the intrinsic binary
  fraction to $f_\mathrm{bin} =
  \fbincorr^{+\fbinerrup}_{-\fbinerrdn}$ and the period-distribution
  exponent to $\pi = \pibestfit^{+\pierrup}_{-\pierrdn}$.
